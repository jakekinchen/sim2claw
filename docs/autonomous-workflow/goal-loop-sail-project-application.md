- The 1% joint and end-effector RMS guards remain binding.

  outcomes or one strict task success; this loop cannot weaken it.

  force, object dynamics, or transfer result.

## Stop rule

The loop ends with either an evaluator-admitted task/mechanism advancement or
an honest terminal negative after the frozen budget. Missing physical geometry
or measurement may be named as a blocker, but cannot be imputed.
