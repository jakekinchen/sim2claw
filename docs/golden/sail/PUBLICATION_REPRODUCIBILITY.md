# Publication Reproducibility Contract

Every reported number must resolve to:

1. source artifact identity and proof class;
2. code commit and runtime lock;
3. frozen config, split, seed, and evaluator identity;
4. ignored generated receipt or tracked small fixture;
5. regeneration command;
6. result-table or figure cell; and
7. claim boundary and negative-result status.

Tables must separate synthetic benchmark, retired retrospective, prospective
simulator, learned-policy simulation, physical read-only, and physical-task
evidence. Provider attempts may be pooled only when provider, model, revision,
harness, budgets, and tool surface are identical. Sealed results are evaluated
once by the deterministic evaluator after method freeze.

The publication package must include negative controls, ablations, uncertainty,
abstentions, failed hypotheses, exact inclusion/exclusion counts, and resource
closeout. A policy win is optional; a methods claim may not be restated as
physical transfer.
