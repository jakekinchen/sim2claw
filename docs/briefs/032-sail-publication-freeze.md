# Slice Brief 032 - SAIL Publication Freeze and Paper Package

**Date:** 2026-07-22

## Objective

Complete P1-17 and Phase 1 by freezing one receipt-bound publication campaign
that converts the completed deterministic SAIL, seeded benchmark, governed
agent campaign, retained-workcell case, prospective simulator experiment,
TwinWorthiness boundary, and policy terminal negative into a reproducible paper
package. The package must separate every proof class and make unavailable
future-hardware results visible rather than silently omitting them.

## Acceptance Criteria

- All six research questions, ten required ablations, primary metrics,
  confidence procedures, effect sizes, secondary-comparison correction, and
  stop rules are frozen before the publication compiler runs.
- Paired seeded-case statistics are computed from source-bound benchmark bytes;
  retained evidence is resampled only by whole episode; no row-level
  pseudoreplication is introduced.
- Development, public, sealed, retrospective, prospective simulator, provider,
  policy, and future physical results occupy separate tables with explicit
  terminal-negative, abstention, blocked, and not-evaluable states.
- Paper figure/table slots bind the same receipts as Studio and the evaluator;
  future warm-start and hardware slots are marked unavailable, never inferred.
- A machine-readable reproduction map resolves every claim to source artifact,
  digest, command, proof class, evaluator owner, and authority boundary.
- The Phase 2 operator packet freezes identities, roles, measurements, probes,
  predictions, stop conditions, and gateway/authority requirements without
  opening capture or motion.
- All automatic CI tiers, focused publication tests, the broad repository gate,
  JSON/receipt checks, resource closeout, and clean diff checks pass.

## Stop Conditions

- Any sealed label, held-out result, or evaluator-only state becomes visible to
  a training, proposal, or agent condition.
- A missing ablation, statistic, agent comparison, policy result, or physical
  result is represented as a successful zero rather than unavailable evidence.
- Retained physical episodes are resampled below the whole-episode unit.
- Provider attempts with unequal transport/model identity are pooled.
- An output table or figure is produced from an unbound parallel data path.
- Publication compilation changes the simulator, posterior, hypothesis family,
  Phase 2 predictions, golden verdicts, or TwinWorthiness certificate.
- Paid compute, a provider session, a container, a device lease, camera access,
  or robot authority is opened.
