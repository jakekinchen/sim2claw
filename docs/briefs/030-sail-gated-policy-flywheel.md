# Slice Brief 030 - SAIL Gated Policy Self-Improvement Flywheel

**Date:** 2026-07-22

## Objective

Complete P1-15 by making the existing Learning Factory execute the complete
ACT/GR00T improvement loop on synthetic fixtures while the real campaign
remains stopped at its exact-scope TwinWorthiness boundary. Every generated
row, policy comparison, failure route, and corrective suffix must carry enough
lineage for independent replay and evaluator verification.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The fixture lane executes object-/target-relative source compilation,
  posterior-bounded candidate generation, full MuJoCo replay, strict evaluator
  admission, state ACT training, independent policy evaluation,
  counterexample routing, and promotion-state publication.
- The overhead-camera GR00T challenger has a separate, bounded fixture path or
  a deterministic explicit skip with its independent modality, lineage, and
  evaluator contract preserved.
- Generated candidates bind twin, posterior, source segment, teacher,
  simulator, action, task/distribution, and evaluator identities.
- Failed candidates, failed prefixes, held-out failures, and unqualified
  physical-source rows cannot enter training. Only independently successful
  corrective suffixes may be admitted.
- Training and generators cannot issue capabilities or promote themselves.
  The current real project produces zero generated/admitted rows and zero
  comparisons without a matching `TW-DATA`/`TW-SELECTION` envelope.
- The full fixture flywheel, negative controls, GOLD registry, and repository
  gate pass deterministically without new physical observations or paid
  compute.

## Stop Conditions

- Any real lane proceeds from a synthetic certificate or from the retired
  workcell's mismatched certificate.
- Posterior sampling widens into undeclared domain randomization or mutates a
  retained source action trace.
- A failed candidate, failed prefix, held-out failure, or evaluator-private
  state becomes an imitation row.
- ACT and GR00T proof classes, inputs, evaluators, or claims are conflated.
- Training, simulated reward, a checkpoint, or a generator promotes itself.
- Hardware, a new physical observation, or unbounded paid compute is used.
